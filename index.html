Fun Facts!

  1. Lacrosse is one of the fastest growing sports in the United States
  2. Indian lacrosse was initially played with 100 to 1000 players!
  3. Lacrosse was originally intended to toughen up young men for war
  4. In the Summer of 1763, 2 tribes played lacrosse to distract British soldiers allowing them to recapture Fort Michilimackinac
