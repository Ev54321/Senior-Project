My Personal Experience Playing Lacrosse

How I started playing lacrosse

I first heard about lacrosse from a close friend of mine in 4th grade. A couple weeks later my parents and I had decided to go to a lacrosse clinic to see if I would enjoy it. While there I got to meet a lot of new people and had a lot of fun playing. That day, when I got home I told my parents that I really enjoyed the clinic and that I wanted to go to another one if possible. After going to a couple more I had decided that lacrosse was the sport that I really wanted to play.

That same year I joined the Hillsboro Area Lacrosse Club (HALO) where I met many of my current friends and teamates. Are club wasn't exactly the best club, we would usually have a losing record by the end of the season, but we still had a lot of fun playing together and learning more about lacrosse. As we got older we were taught the different positions and I grew to really enjoy playing defense and long stick middle (LSM).

In my Freshman year of highschool I expirienced, for the first time what it felt like to be apart of a winning sports team where we went 11 - 9 and went all the way to the cascade cup finals. Even thought we lost, the expirience of going to the finals and playing a much better team at a more competetive level fueled my love for the sport even more.
