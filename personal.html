My Personal Experience Playing Lacrosse

How I started playing lacrosse

I first heard about lacrosse from a close friend of mine in 4th grade. A couple weeks later my parents and I had decided to go to a lacrosse clinic to see if I would enjoy it. While there I got to meet a lot of new people and had a lot of fun playing. That day, when I got home I told my parents that I wanted to try and play lacrosse for the year.
