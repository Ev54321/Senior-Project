How we are run

Although the sport is seeing a massive increase in popularity across the world, here at Century High School we are still not an official school sport and are instead a sports club.

Due to this we do not recieve funding from the school so all our funding is from players, family and friends. We do a couple fundraisers throughout the season and run consessions during our home games to help make money for the team.
